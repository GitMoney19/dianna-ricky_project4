Rules of the Game

Objective: Be the first to get rid of all your cards!

  • You can only play a card that matches in value or suit as the top card in the discard pile unless it's an 8.

  • TWOs played means the next players must pickup 2.

  • JACKS will skip a turn

  • EIGHTs are wild cards and can be played anytime. They also allow the player to decide the next suit.

  • More than one card can be played if the value of the pair/triplet of cards matches the suit or value in the discard pile.

X

Special Cards

Eights of any suit are wild cards and can be played at anytime.

The player playing the card will choose what suit the next player will have to follow.

Queen of Spades: Next player must pick up 5 cards.

Two of any suit: Next player must pick up 2 cards.

If the next player also plays a two, the following player will have to pick up four. Twos have cumulative effects.
