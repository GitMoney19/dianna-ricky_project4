Rules of the Game

Objective: Be the first player to be rid of all the cards in your hand

  • You can only play a card that is matching in either value or suit as the card in the centre unless you have an 8 in any suit.

  • When a player plays a "2" card, the next player must pick up two cards.

  • When a player plays a "Jack" card, the next player will miss a turn.

  • The "8" card of any suit is a wild card, and it can be played at anytime regardless of the value/suit in the discard pile. The player who played the 8 will get to choose which suit the next player must play.

  • You can play more than one card if the value of the pair/triplet of cards matches the suit or value.

Special Cards

Eights of any suit are wild cards and can be played at any time regardless of what the card is in the discard pile.

The player playing the card will choose what suit the next player will have to follow.

When the Queen of Spades is played, the player whose turn is next will have to pick up five cards.

When a two of any suit is played, the player who's turn is next will have to pick up two cards.

However, if the next player also has a two, the following player will have to pick up four. Twos have cumulative effects.
